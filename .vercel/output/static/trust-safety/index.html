Safety Isn’t Optional.
It’s Our Foundation.

In Florida, tree work is high-stakes. We’ve built a network where licensing, insurance, and elite arboricultural standards are the bare minimum.

The ProTreeTrim™ Vetting Protocol

Most homeowners realize too late that an unlicensed "guy with a chainsaw" is a massive legal and financial liability. Florida law is strict, and property damage during tree removal can cost tens of thousands. Our mission is to filter the elite from the amateurs.

🛡️

Rigid Insurance Verification

We don't just take a contractor’s word for it. Our network members must maintain active General Liability and Workers' Compensation insurance specific to tree services. This protects you from being held liable for onsite injuries or accidental property damage.

📜

State & Local Compliance

Tree care regulations vary by Florida county. Our vetting process ensures that the specialists dispatched to your property understand local permit requirements and adhere to Florida’s strict arboricultural statutes.

🏗️

ANSI A300 Performance Standards

We advocate for the life of your trees. Our partners are expected to follow ANSI A300 standards—the industry-recognized gold standard for tree care. This means no improper "topping" or "lion's tailing" that weakens your trees before hurricane season.

🌪️

Hurricane Readiness & Response

Florida’s climate is unforgiving. We prioritize crews that possess high-capacity rigging and heavy machinery capable of handling storm-damaged Oaks and Palms safely, minimizing further risk to your home or power lines.

Our "Zero Compromise" Commitment

If a service provider in our network fails to meet our safety benchmarks or receives consistent negative feedback regarding professional conduct, they are immediately removed from our dispatch list. We represent the homeowner first.
