## Current-source checkpoints

### Tree-health and diagnostic boundaries

All 10 articles are useful tree-health or visible-symptom topics, but they need stronger non-diagnostic boundaries. The revision package should preserve helpful homeowner pattern recognition while making clear that symptoms do not prove disease, pest identity, removability, or structural safety without an onsite evaluation.

### Source normalization

Most pages lack a clean source section. Revision should add or normalize source sections with authoritative Florida or university/extension references where possible and avoid unsupported treatment, pest, disease, or structural claims.

### Service routes and CTA

These pages are not purely informational. Many lead naturally to professional evaluation, tree risk assessment, trimming, removal, emergency service, or palm/tree-care decisions. Add direct service-route links where natural, but avoid turning every disease article into a hard-sell page.
