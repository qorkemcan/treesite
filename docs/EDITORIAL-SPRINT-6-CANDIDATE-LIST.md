## Package logic

### Package 1 — Legacy risk, storm, legal, pruning, cost, and preservation hubs
These eight pages sit closest to emergency calls, risk decisions, pruning/removal choices, neighbor incidents, mature-tree estimates, and relocation alternatives.

### Package 2 — Vines, owner authority, pruning timing, roots, and stump completion
These pages address DIY risk, permit authority, root damage, replanting, stump chemicals, and long-term root behavior.

### Package 3 — Visual symptoms, water stress, and identification hub
These pages can attract broad homeowner traffic while preventing remote overdiagnosis and unnecessary removal.

### Package 4 — Florida invasive-tree identification
These pages need current status, exact identification, regional limits, safe removal, disposal, herbicide/licensing, stump, permit, and replacement guidance.
