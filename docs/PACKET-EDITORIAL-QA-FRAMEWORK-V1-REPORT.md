# Packet: Editorial QA Framework v1

## Objective

Created a reusable editorial quality framework for ProTreeTrim's weekly article review process.

This packet is documentation only. It does not rewrite existing articles and does not change UI, pages, routing, navigation, metadata, schemas, or application behavior.

## Files Created

- `docs/EDITORIAL-QA-FRAMEWORK.md`
- `docs/EDITORIAL-QA-CHECKLIST.md`
- `docs/EDITORIAL-QA-SCORECARD.md`
- `docs/PACKET-EDITORIAL-QA-FRAMEWORK-V1-REPORT.md`

## Framework Coverage

The framework defines:

- Purpose of the editorial QA process.
- Weekly review workflow for approximately 30 articles.
- Priority order for selecting articles.
- Review philosophy for natural US English, homeowner-first guidance, decision support, avoiding keyword stuffing, and avoiding AI-sounding content.

## Checklist Coverage

The reusable checklist includes:

- Headline
- Intro
- Search intent
- Short answer
- Decision support
- Natural American English
- AI-pattern review
- Terminology review
- Florida context
- Internal links
- CTA
- Image opportunity
- Schema review
- Overall consistency

## Scorecard Coverage

Created a 100-point scorecard with 10 categories worth 10 points each:

- Headline
- Intro
- Readability
- US English
- AI detection risk
- Florida relevance
- Decision support
- Internal links
- CTA
- Overall polish

The scorecard includes result bands:

- 95-100: Excellent
- 90-94: Minor polish
- 80-89: Needs revision
- Below 80: Major rewrite

## Review Template

The scorecard includes the requested example review template:

- Article
- Category
- Score
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Recommended edits
- Image prompt needed?
- Internal links to add
- Final status
- Publish
- Minor polish
- Major revision
