## Package logic

### Package 1 — Insurance, claims, appraisal, and property transactions
The first eight pages sit closest to post-loss decisions, emergency payments, claim evidence, property value, real-estate due diligence, pre-listing work, and recurring absentee-owner service.

### Package 2 — Neighbor, boundary, HOA-root, sidewalk, utility, and waterfront control
These pages require strong property-control, legal, public-authority, and physical-tree-work boundaries.

### Package 3 — Surveys, local enforcement, seasonal restrictions, and immediate safety
These pages address formal records, local preservation, unauthorized work, nesting/timing questions, DIY hazards, emergency exclusion, family safety, and power-line coordination.

### Package 4 — Coastal structures, wildfire, erosion, storm scope, lightning, and clearing
The final six connect engineering and environmental constraints with high-value emergency, removal, and land-clearing decisions.
