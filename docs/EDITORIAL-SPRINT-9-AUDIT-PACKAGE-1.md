### Closed tree wound with internal decay

This article has a strong premise but currently has older structure and potential homeowner diagnostic overreach.

Needed improvements:

- separate wound closure, compartmentalization, cavities, conks, cracks, oozing, old pruning cuts, targets, and risk review,
- add direct service routes and source section,

- preserve the UF/IFAS nitrogen-immobilization nuance.
