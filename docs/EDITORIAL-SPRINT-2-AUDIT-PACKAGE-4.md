### 811 and private facilities

- The current 811 article contains the core rule but has no official source links.
- Florida law generally requires notice at least two full business days before excavation, subject to narrow exemptions.
- The single-family homeowner exemption is limited to work entirely on qualifying property, no deeper than ten inches, with due care and without encroaching on a member operator's right-of-way, easement, or permitted use.
- Sunshine 811 expressly directs homeowners to call before grinding or pulling a stump.
- Member operators mark facilities they own and maintain; irrigation, septic, pool lines, landscape lighting, and other private facilities may require separate locating.
- The revision should add ticket preparation, positive response, marks, tolerance-zone awareness, and what to do when private facilities may conflict.

### Stump-content overlap
